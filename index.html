The world through the eyes of teenagers

As we, teenagers are sitting in our rooms, as we hear our parents listening to the news, as we check social media, hoping for a glimpse of hope, breaking the pattern of the darkness around us, did we ever wonder if that darkness had an impact on ourselves? On our feelings? On our daily routine? Sometimes, we get bored, we start to do something, we sing, or go for a walk, study, sleep, and sometimes write. Write about what we saw, how did we see it? Something we heard; how did we hear it? About a fictional love story, we wish experiencing it. About words, we made into a melody. We write, because we can, through our writings, modify perceptions, and visions about our surroundings. We write, because this is proof, of what our generation went through, before blooming, as we hope we will. In this book, conceived by Next Big Thing who’s objective #1 was to bring to light, diverse Lebanese talents, is a glimpse, of our generation’s “New York best-sellers”. Close the door, open your eyes, and start reading…
