When People Ask Me

When people ask me:” What happened between the two of you?”
I suddenly become mute, I lose every ability of speech
My lips become entirely incapable of articulating any letter, any word
Because the 2 year passageway leading up to today immediately fades away, disappears
I do not know what we are
I do not even recognize what we were
I am unable to express the bitterness that is now forever engraved in my heart
The hysteria my cerebrum undergoes every time someone yells your name
It’s something I find to be a lot worse than unfinished business
Because sometimes I find myself unable to glue the glass back together
All we are now, is one useless chapter in both our stories.
