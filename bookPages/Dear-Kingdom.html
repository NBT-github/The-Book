Dear Kingdom

Look where the hills meet the valley, Where the sun and the moon kiss. Where everyone is so happy, And Baptised with Heavenly bliss. Go to the domes that touch the sky, And listen to the angels’ song. Golden fires will take you high, To a place you truly belong. Roaming above iron and blood, Don’t ever look down brave fighter, Or you will drown in the red flood. When the hands of time are tighter, In the mortal world or divine; I’m proud that this Kingdom is mine.
